Our Story

Crafting Unforgettable Maritime Experiences

At Panther Maritime, we believe every journey on the water should be extraordinary. Based in the beautiful shores of Fajardo, Puerto Rico, we specialize in curated boat tours that combine adventure with luxury.

From intimate sunset cruises to island-hopping adventures, each experience includes gourmet picnic spreads prepared fresh for your voyage. And with our complimentary airport pickup service, your Caribbean escape begins the moment you land.
